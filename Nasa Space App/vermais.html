ESTU

Our project aims to tackle one of humanity's greatest challenges: the waste of essential resources. The proposal is to optimize the use of materials during a space mission to Mars, utilizing waste generated throughout the process. The mission, lasting about three years, inevitably generates waste. Our solution consists of repurposing materials that would be discarded by constructing external structures to the habitat. The ESTU (External Support Structure), as it is called, Our project aims to reduce waste, ensuring greater efficiency in resource use. In a world where space exploration is growing exponentially, it is necessary to establish ways to prevent the increase of waste in space, thus solidifying humanity's commitment to sustainability in the cosmos.
